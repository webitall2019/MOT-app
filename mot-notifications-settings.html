INFO
What is 1-st Reminder?

On the first day of Reminder this will be sent to the customer (If the Text MOT communication allowed). In this case customer will have 24 hours to reply in sms form. Otherwise Duty of care notification will be sent (if duty of care turned on).

If there will be no reply from customer in 24 hours, the 2-nd Reminder will be activated.
